What is EcoMap?

Our Project

We are a group of high-school students/friends that have a strong desire to raise awareness of one of the most pressing problems going on throughout the world: the loss of natural habitats due to environmental issues we humans are partly responsible for creating. We combined this desire with our passion for technology to develop an online platform which lets users readily identify areas that are in crisis and to do something about the situation.
