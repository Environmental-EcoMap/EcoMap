Our Project

Hi, I'm Nithik and I go to Independence High School in Frisco, Texas. I have a strong interest in technology and service. An rising issue I felt strongly about was the countless ways we humans were harming the Earth and as a result the wildlife inhabiting our planet. In order to take a step forward in remedying some of humanity's devastating effects, I decided to merge my passion for service with my interest in technology to raise awareness through EcoMap.

"Surrounded by deforestation, critically endangered gorillas hang on by a thread"

The Cross River Gorilla is one of the most endangered species of apes in the planet. This species is on the brink of extinction due to the defoerastation of the mountain forests at the Nigeria-Cameroon border. Want to learn more about these poor apes? Click on the image of the gorrila to read an article on the Cross River Gorilla by Mongabay.

"Australia’s Great Barrier Reef under Threat"

The Great Barrier Reef is home to over 1,500 species of fish, 411 types of hard coral, one-third of the world’s soft corals, 134 species of sharks and rays. This great habitat is in danger due to excessive pollution in the water. Click on the image of the turtle to enter an article by World Wilflife if you want to learn more about the Great Barrier Reef's problems and want to take action.
