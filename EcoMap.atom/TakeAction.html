OUR PLANET IS IN NEED

The Problem

Believe it or not, our Earth is suffering. Global warming, deforestation, droughts--these phenomena are merely part of what is continuing to devastate living beings throughout the world. Standing at the root of these issues are humans! To take a step forward toward a chance at redemption, we have created this platform to raise awareness about both the environmental problems occurring and the organizations already working towards a solution. You can also take action by utilizing EcoMap to aide the organizations helping the environment in areas you feel passionate about and become an active collaborator by submitting a problem that you think deserves recognition on our map below. Through your help, our broken world can be mended!

Reach Out to Us!

Use the contact form below to submit a problem that you want in the map, provide feedback on our platform, or reach out to us! We will get back to you as soon as possible!
