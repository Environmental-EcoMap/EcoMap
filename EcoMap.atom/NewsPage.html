"Polar Bears Really Could Go Extinct by 2100!"

A study by the University of Toronto suggests that polar bears could go extinct by the end of the century, and its all due to climate change! The Arctic Species has been struggling immensely to adapt to the consequenses of our actions and the situation is on the vergee of a breaking point. "Severe Plastic Pollution in the River Thames"

The River Thames in England has the greatest recorded amount of microplastics compared to any other river! Along with many plastic particles accumulating on shorelines, plastic has been found in the body of crabs living in the river! This pollution can severely affect the organisms that call the river home. To learn more, click on the plastic image title to read an article by BBC News.

"Controversial EIA Draft to Loosen Environmental Regulations In India"

The Government of India has proposed a new set of policies on the country's environmental clearance regime on industrial projects. The draft Environmental Impact Assesment 2020 (EIA) heavily favors industry and does away with many important restrictions to potect the environment! "Siberia's Lengthy Heatwave Impossible Without Climate Change, Scientists Says"

Siberia's lengthy heatwave recently hit a record breaking high of 100.4 degrees. This heatwave has been responsible for starting fires that have burnt millions of acres. However, a recent analysis led by the U.K.'s Met office found that the heatwave proabably wouldn't have happened if it wasn't for human-caused climate change!
